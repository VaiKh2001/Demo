Group Classes: Workout Fun and Effectively with Like-minded People

Group classes at FitnessClub are the perfect way to combine the benefits of working out with the fun of socialising. We offer a variety of programmes to suit all fitness levels, and our experienced trainers create dynamic and motivating classes that will energise and motivate you.

A Variety of Programmes for Everyone: At our gym, we understand that every person is unique, so we offer a wide range of group classes so that you can choose what you really enjoy and what suits your goals:


Yoga is not only a way to improve flexibility and strengthen your body, but also an opportunity to find inner balance and relieve stress. Our classes include various yoga styles such as Hatha, Vinyasa and Power Yoga. No matter your fitness level, you will be able to enjoy a practice that will help you relax, improve your posture and focus.

Zumba is an energetic dance workout that combines elements of Latin dance and cardio. To rhythmic music, you will perform movements that not only burn calories, but also lift your spirits. This is a great activity for those who want to have fun while exercising and get a boost of positive emotions.

Functional training at FitnessClub is aimed at developing strength, endurance, flexibility and coordination. Classes include exercises with your own weight, kettlebells, TRX loops and other equipment that helps to work out all muscle groups. This type of training is useful for everyday life, as it improves overall fitness and helps in performing daily tasks.



Nutrition Consultations: The Key to Your Fitness Goals

At FitnessClub, we understand that proper nutrition is the foundation of your success in achieving health and fitness. Whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle mass, improve your stamina or simply maintain a balanced diet, our experts will help you create a personalized plan that will provide powerful support in achieving your goals.
